Musings

Hot Sugar's music is called "associative music, where he pulls in a ton of sounds from instruments, found sounds, and non-traditional places to sample them into his own melodies." He has been perfecting the technique since the age of 13. He described associative music as "try[ing] to capture sounds the same way a photographer would capture an image: If something looks poetic, I'll record it".
